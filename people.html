DBWeb Team at Télécom ParisTech

Data managed by information systems are more and more complex, distributed, heterogeneous, dynamic, and of various forms. DBWeb is a team of researchers at Télécom ParisTech who study the fundamental issues raised in modern data and knowledge management systems, especially on the Web or in collaborative contexts oriented towards peer-to-peer networks. Research interests cover theoretical foundations of database management systems, practical solutions and applications, as well as cognitive aspects.

The DBWeb team is part of the IC2 group, the Computer science & Networks department, and the LTCI laboratory of the CNRS.
